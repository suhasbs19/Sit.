Project Title
Health AI — AI-Powered Symptom Checker & Urgency Detector

Problem Statement
Many people experience symptoms like fever, chest pain, headache, or breathing difficulty but do not know whether the condition is minor or requires immediate medical attention. This confusion often leads to delayed treatment, self-medication, panic, or unnecessary hospital visits. Access to quick and reliable first-level health guidance is limited, especially in urgent situations. 

Description
Health AI is a smart healthcare web application that helps users understand their symptoms instantly using Artificial Intelligence. Users can type or speak their symptoms in natural language, and the system analyzes them using Google Gemini AI. It predicts possible health conditions, provides urgency level (Low / Medium / High / Emergency), gives general advice, and recommends the right medical specialist.


The application also includes emergency alerts, voice symptom input, downloadable PDF reports, responsive design for mobile/desktop, and proper medical disclaimers. It is built with a React frontend and Flask backend. 

Proposed Solution
Develop an AI-powered symptom analysis platform that provides quick preliminary health guidance based on user symptoms. The system uses machine learning/NLP to detect possible illnesses, estimate risk level, and guide users toward the next best action such as home care, doctor consultation, or emergency help. This saves time, improves awareness, and supports faster healthcare decisions. 

Tech Stack (optional)
Frontend: React + Vite
Backend: Flask
AI Model: Google Gemini 1.5 Flash
Deployment: Vercel + Render
